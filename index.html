About the Journal

Journal of Regional Anesthesia & Ultrasound (JRAU) is an international open-access journal dedicated to regional anesthesia, ultrasound-guided procedures, perioperative pain medicine, and innovative anesthetic techniques.

The journal publishes original research articles, reviews, technical reports, cadaveric studies, educational innovations, and AI-assisted applications in regional anesthesia.

Aims & Scope

  • Regional anesthesia
  • Ultrasound-guided nerve blocks
  • Fascial plane blocks
  • Perioperative ultrasound
  • Pain medicine
  • AI-assisted regional anesthesia
  • Cadaveric and anatomical studies
  • Educational innovations
