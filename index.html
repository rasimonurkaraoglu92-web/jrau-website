About the Journal

Journal of Regional Anesthesia & Ultrasound (JRAU) is an international, peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to regional anesthesia, ultrasound-guided procedures, perioperative pain medicine, and innovation in anesthetic practice.

JRAU aims to provide a focused platform for clinicians, researchers, educators, and trainees working in ultrasound-guided regional anesthesia and related perioperative fields.

Aims & Scope

Regional Anesthesia

Peripheral nerve blocks, neuraxial techniques, fascial plane blocks, and perioperative analgesia.

Ultrasound-Guided Procedures

Sonoanatomy, procedural safety, needle visualization, and image-guided interventions.

Innovation & Education

AI-assisted blocks, simulation, teaching methods, cadaveric studies, and clinical workflows.

Publication Ethics

JRAU follows international standards for publication ethics, peer review, authorship transparency, conflict of interest disclosure, data integrity, and responsible use of artificial intelligence in scientific writing.

All submitted manuscripts are expected to comply with ethical principles including appropriate ethics committee approval, informed consent when applicable, plagiarism avoidance, and transparent reporting.
